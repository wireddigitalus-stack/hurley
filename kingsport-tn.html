Kingsport — Industrial Strength, Growing Opportunity

Kingsport, Tennessee is the industrial backbone of the Tri-Cities region. Anchored by Eastman Chemical Company — one of the largest employers in the state — Kingsport offers a stable, diversified economic base that makes it an attractive market for commercial real estate investment.

The city's growing medical corridor, expanding retail scene along Stone Drive, and strong residential demand from Eastman's workforce create consistent opportunities for Hurley Enterprise in both the commercial and residential acquisition markets.

We buy residential properties across Kingsport — in any condition, all cash, close in as few as 14 days. And for commercial investors, we can identify and acquire off-market opportunities throughout the city.
