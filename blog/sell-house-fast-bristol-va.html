The traditional real estate process (list, stage, show, wait, negotiate, wait some more) takes an average of 60–90 days in the best markets. For homeowners facing a life transition, that timeline is simply not realistic.

Will I Get a Fair Price?

This is the most common question we get — and it deserves a straight answer. A cash offer from Hurley Enterprise will typically be below what you might receive at full retail value after a 90-day listing process — because we're taking on risk, covering repairs, and providing certainty of close that a traditional buyer can't. That's the trade-off.

But when you account for the realtor commission (5–6%), seller-paid closing costs (2–3%), holding costs during a 90-day listing period, and any repairs the buyer's inspection demands — many sellers net more from a cash sale than they expect. We encourage every seller to run the numbers transparently, and we're happy to walk through that with you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to clean the house out before selling?

No. You can take what you want and leave the rest. We handle cleanout as part of our process. Many estate sellers find this the biggest relief — not having to deal with decades of belongings under emotional circumstances.

Can I still get a cash offer if the house has unpaid taxes or liens?

Yes, in most cases. We can often structure the purchase to pay off outstanding liens at closing. Call us and we'll tell you exactly what's possible for your specific situation.

How do you determine the offer price?

We look at comparable recent sales in your area, the property's condition, what repairs or improvements we'd make, and current market conditions. We're transparent about our math — if you want to understand how we arrived at a number, just ask.

Is there any obligation after I get an offer?

None. Our cash offers are 100% no-obligation. You can take it, decline it, think about it for a week — whatever you need. We'll never pressure you.
