Bristol TN Market

Downtown Bristol TN — A City on the Rise

Bristol, Tennessee is experiencing its most significant economic transformation in decades. The opening of Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Bristol — just across the state line in Bristol VA — has supercharged demand for commercial space, hospitality, retail, and professional services on both sides of State Street.

With a downtown revitalization underway, growing restaurant and entertainment scene, and increasing business migration from larger MSAs, Bristol TN offers some of the most compelling commercial investment fundamentals in the entire Appalachian region.

Hurley Enterprise LLC is the leading commercial real estate firm operating in this market — with flagship developments on both sides of the state line and deep relationships across the business community.
