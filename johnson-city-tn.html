Johnson City — University Power & Medical Momentum

Johnson City, Tennessee is the largest city in the Tri-Cities MSA and home to East Tennessee State University (ETSU) and the Ballad Health medical system. This combination creates a dynamic, recession-resistant real estate market with strong rental demand, active downtown retail, and continuous commercial expansion.

The downtown Johnson City district has seen significant investment in recent years — new restaurants, boutique retail, and mixed-use developments along Market Street and the surrounding blocks. Meanwhile, the medical corridor along Veterans Way continues to attract healthcare-adjacent commercial development.

Hurley Enterprise is an active buyer across Johnson City — residential properties, commercial buildings, and land. If you're ready to sell, we're ready to buy.
