Our Story

An Entrepreneurial-Based Enterprise

Hurley Enterprise is different — our team takes a hands-on approach, taking time to understand each client's needs and budget, then building a comprehensive plan to execute. We have a proven track record successfully managing millions of dollars in assets, construction and development projects.

Holding over 1 million square feet of commercial and residential property, we definitely know our way around the real estate world. We use our experience to attack each project from multiple angles — leveraging decades of expertise to simplify the process for our clients at the best value, with lightning fast speed.

Whether that's helping a seller close fast on a property, finding a business the perfect location, or delivering a ground-up development project — we go the extra mile because we believe that's simply part of the job.

"People do business with People — and Said Done means exactly what it says."

— J. Allen Hurley II, CEO & President
